About Us

Welcome to SAFAR, where we bring the road to your doorstep with style, convenience, and unparalleled service. Our journey began with a shared passion for travel and a vision to take exploring the world more accessible and exciting. Founded by a team of dedicated and enthusiastic individuals Sujal, Udita, Sagar and Amit - we are committed to providing you with the ultimate car rental experience.

  • Udita: Our customer service superstar, Udita is dedicated to making your experience smooth and enjoyable, ready to axsist with any questions you might have.
  • Sagar: 5-star Rated Driver, Sagar's knowledge of cars and the industry ensures that we offer the best vehicles for your journeys

  • Amit: Amit, Car Owner brings his passion for travel and business acumen to the team, striving to make SAFAR a leader in the Industry.

Our Mission

At SAFAR, we aim to revolutionize the way you explore and experience new destinations. Our mission is to offer top-quality car rentals that fit your budget, provide exceptional customer service, and ensure your road trips are memorable and worry-free
